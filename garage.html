Workshop


At Handlebar we have a fully equipped workshop, including a bike wash.
Here you can do everything from an engine renovation to a simple mirror adjustment. Drills, workbenches, lifts... it's all here!

Storage


Over 3000ft² of floor space certainly helps.
Shelving all your parts, oils, tools and whatnots is now a problem of the past. Your bike will have ample personal space, and left completely untouched.

Hangout


In our hangout area we have a coffee machine, snacks, magazines and a bar.
Sometimes we eat here, sometimes we sleep here. There's even the ocassional party! It's essentially our meditation chamber.

How does it work?

If you want to store and work on your bike, we strongly suggest you swing by or contact us through any form provided at the bottom of the site. Since Handlebar is run by a small group of motorcycle enthusiasts, we rely heavily on everyone contributing. That includes everything from cleaning to organizing events. The only costs attached to having a spot here are general maintenance and bills.

Rides and the hangout are always open and free to everyone. We only ask that you come with a happy demeanor.
